    Ice cream is a sweetened frozen food typically eaten as a snack or dessert. It may be made from dairy milk or cream and is flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and a spice, such as cocoa or vanilla, or with fruit such as strawberries or peaches. It can also be made by whisking a flavored cream base and liquid nitrogen together.

    Iced coffee is a coffee beverage served cold. It may be prepared either by brewing coffee in the normal way and then serving it over ice or in cold milk, or by brewing the coffee cold. In hot brewing, sweeteners and flavoring may be added before cooling, as they dissolve faster. Iced coffee can also be sweetened with pre-dissolved sugar in water.

    A milkshake (sometimes simply called a shake) is a sweet drink made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings or sweeteners such as butterscotch, caramel sauce, chocolate syrup, fruit syrup, or whole fruit into a thick, sweet, cold mixture. Our company has its own raw material base and pays special attention to the parameters of the processed raw materials. The company carries out maximum quality control both at the stage of milk procurement and at production.

how it’s made?

Cows graze in the meadows of the Ukrainian open spaces and the meadows of the Carpathians in summer. In winter, they are fed with fragrant silage and hay.

Our company has its own raw material base and pays special attention to the parameters of the processed raw materials. The company carries out maximum quality control both at the stage of milk procurement and at production.

The best milk makes the best ice cream.

The blast chiller provides an outlet temperature of up to -30ºC, which improves the appearance and quality of ice cream during further packaging and storage. Modern German equipment Big Drum enables the company to offer exclusive ice cream to the Ukrainian consumer.
