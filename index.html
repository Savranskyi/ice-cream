how it’s made?

Cows graze in the meadows of the Ukrainian open spaces and the meadows of the Carpathians in summer. In winter, they are fed with fragrant silage and hay.

Our company has its own raw material base and pays special attention to the parameters of the processed raw materials. The company carries out maximum quality control both at the stage of milk procurement and at production.

The best milk makes the best ice cream.
